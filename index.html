Hi, I'm Dave...

Hello! I'm Dave. I'm a software developer, a regular conference speaker and author of SARB (Static Analysis Results Baseliner).

I'm a director and developer at Lamp Bristol, a software consultancy that specialises in web applications. We make your tech behind your ideas; whether that be a start up idea or a a tool to make your business more efficient, we can help you implement it. Drop me an email to find out more.

I've been a software developer in the tech industry since 2002. I've got commercial experience of several languages including C, C++, Java, Python and PHP. Find out more on my LinkedIn profile.

On the community side I organise PHP-SW and Bristol PHP Training. If you live in Bristol or nearby then do sign up to these events. They are a great way learn more and meet others in the PHP community.

I speak at PHP meetups throughout the UK and in 2018 I started speaking at conferences around the world.

When I'm not coding you'll probably find me running with Southville Running club. Or underwater. The photo above is of me in the Scilly Isles.
